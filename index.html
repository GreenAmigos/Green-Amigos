Green Amigos

Green Amigos is a startup company that spreads awareness among people using initial level segregation and collection while bridging the gap between demand and supply of the waste paper hence reducing the import of waste paper from different countries. Founded by Kamal Ashish and Anshit Pathak , a duo having experience in waste paper management.Green Amigos will create a supply chain between people and industries requiring raw material giving chance to small rag pickers headquartered in Allahabad, U.P. Currently trying to set foot in Allahabad, the company is projecting to cover maximum number of organizations producing waste paper. Plans for expansion includes setting of warehouses for inventory in Allahabad, one of the most populated cities where the company aims to gain greater market penetration.
